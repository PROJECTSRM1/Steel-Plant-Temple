Types of Darshan

  • General Darshan: Open to all devotees with a queue system for smooth flow.
  • Special / Seva Darshan: Priority entry with shorter waiting times. Can be booked online or at the temple office.
  • Festival Darshan: Special arrangements during Mandala Pooja, Makara Jyothi, and Utsavam celebrations.
  • Virtual Darshan: Online streaming for devotees unable to visit in person.

Online Darshan Booking

To ensure a comfortable experience and manage crowd flow, devotees are encouraged to book Darshan tickets online.

  1. Visit the Pooja Services page.
  2. Select your preferred Darshan type and date.
  3. Complete the booking form and payment process.
  4. Receive your e-ticket and instructions via email or SMS.

Online Darshan booking is subject to availability and temple regulations.

Dress Code

  • Men: Dhoti/Mundu (shirt and vest to be removed before entering sanctum).
  • Women: Saree, salwar kameez, or modest traditional wear.
  • Children: Neat, comfortable clothing.

Devotees wearing non-traditional or revealing clothes may not be permitted inside the sanctum.

Darshan Guidelines for Devotees

  • Arrive at least 30 minutes before your scheduled Darshan time.
  • Deposit footwear, mobile phones, and cameras at designated counters.
  • Maintain silence and devotion in the temple premises.
  • Offer coconuts, flowers, and prasadam only at approved counters.
  • Follow instructions from temple volunteers and staff.
